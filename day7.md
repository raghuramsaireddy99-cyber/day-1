# WordPress Learning – 5 Step Summary (Day 1 to Day 5)

 Overview
This document summarizes my 5-day learning experience in WordPress, organized into five key steps covering understanding, setup, customization, optimization, and documentation.

---

##  Step 1: Understanding WordPress Basics
Learned the core concepts of WordPress as a Content Management System (CMS).

- What is WordPress and how it works
- Difference between posts and pages
- Overview of dashboard, media, and settings
- Introduction to themes and plugins

---

##  Step 2: Setting Up WordPress
Configured and prepared a working WordPress website.

- Installed WordPress (local/server)
- Configured general settings (site title, permalink)
- Created basic pages and posts
- Installed and activated themes and plugins

---

##  Step 3: Customizing Website Design
Improved the look and feel of the website.

- Customized themes (colors, fonts, layout)
- Created menus and navigation
- Added widgets (search, categories, recent posts)
- Adjusted settings for better user experience

---

##  Step 4: Testing and Optimization
Ensured the website works properly and efficiently.

- Tested all pages and links
- Fixed layout and plugin issues
- Improved website speed and responsiveness
- Checked mobile compatibility

---

##  Step 5: Documentation and Final Output
Documented the process and reviewed the final result.

- Recorded all setup and customization steps
- Prepared usage instructions
- Reviewed website functionality
- Demonstrated final working website

---

##  Conclusion
Over 5 days, I learned how to build, customize, and manage a WordPress website from scratch, including setup, design, optimization, and documentation.
